Why we buildAction first

We grew up on arcade reflexes and console classics — games where skill matters and every run teaches you something. That’s still what pulls us in: readable threats, responsive controls, and difficulty that feels fair because the rules never cheat.

Web games sit right in that sweet spot: instant play, room for mastery, and a huge audience discovering your work for the first time. We’d love to bring that mindset to Poki’s players.

We’re not chasing trends — we’re chasing clarity in motion: one core mechanic, tuned until it sings.
